About us

Advanced Contracting Company is a Saudi Council-approved firm offering recruitment, manpower supply, construction subcontracting, and building materials trading. We provide reliable, compliant, and timely solutions that support large-scale development projects across the region.

With a strong network and expertise across multiple trades, we deliver workforce and contracting services that meet international standards. Our focus on quality and efficiency ensures lasting value for every client.

  • Our Vision

    To be the leading contracting and manpower solutions provider in the region, recognized for delivering reliable workforce, innovative services, and quality-driven infrastructure support that empower industries and communities to grow.

  • Our Mission

    Our mission is to provide compliant, efficient, and value-driven recruitment, subcontracting, and material supply services. Through strong partnerships, skilled manpower, and international standards, we ensure timely project delivery, lasting client satisfaction, and sustainable growth across all sectors we serve.
